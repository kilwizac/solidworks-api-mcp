Public Properties
  Name Description
AccurateEnvLighting Gets or sets whether to use the accurate environment lighting calculations from the high dynamic range imaging (HDRI) scene environment.  
AspectRatio Gets the aspect ratio of the scene floor.  
BackgroundBottomGradientColor Gets or sets the bottom color of the graduated range of background colors of this scene.  
BackgroundBrightness Gets or sets the brightness of the background.  
BackgroundEnvImage Gets or sets the environment image of this scene.  
BackgroundImage Gets or sets the background image for this scene.  
BackgroundTopGradientColor Gets or sets the background color of this scene.  
BackgroundType Gets or sets the type of background for this scene.  
EnvironmentRotation Gets or sets the rotation of the environment image of this scene.  
EnvironmentSize Gets or sets the size of the high dynamic range imaging (HDRI) scene sphere that surrounds the model.  
FitToSWWindow Gets or sets whether to stretch an image to fit the SOLIDWORKS window.  
FixedAspectRatio Gets or sets whether to fix the aspect ratio of the scene floor.  
FlattenFloor Get or sets whether to flatten the floor of a spherical environment to improve the look of models that naturally rest on flat floors.  
FloorAlignment Gets or sets the plane with which to align the scene floor.  
FloorAutoSize Gets or sets whether to resize the scene floor based on the model bounding box.  
FloorDepth Gets or sets the depth of the scene floor.  
FloorDirection Gets or sets whether to flip the direction of the floor of this scene.  
FloorOffset Gets or sets the height of the model above or below the floor of this scene.  
FloorOffsetDirection Gets or sets whether to swap the positions of the scene floor and the model.  
FloorReflections Gets or sets whether to show reflections of the model on the scene floor.  
FloorRotation Gets or sets the rotation of the scene floor relative to the environment of this scene.  
FloorSelection Gets or sets the floor appearance of this scene.  
FloorShadows Gets or sets whether to show shadows cast by the model on the scene floor.  
FloorWidth Gets or sets the width of the scene floor.  
HorizonHeight Gets or sets the height on the high dynamic range imaging (HDRI) scene sphere where the scene floor starts to flatten.  
KeepBackground Gets or sets whether to retain the background when replacing the scene.  
RenderingBrightness Gets or sets the brightness contributed by the high dynamic range imaging (HDRI) environment.  
SceneReflectivity Gets or sets the amount of reflectivity provided by the high dynamic range imaging (HDRI) environment.
