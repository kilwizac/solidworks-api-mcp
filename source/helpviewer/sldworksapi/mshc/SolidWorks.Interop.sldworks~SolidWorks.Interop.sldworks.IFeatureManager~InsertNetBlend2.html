Remarks

Use IModelDocExtension::SelectByID2 and the following selection marks to select the entities for a boundary feature or boundary surface feature.

  • Sketch curves
    • Direction 1: 8193, 16385, 24577
    • Direction 2: 8194, 16386, 24578
  • Faces
    • Direction 1: 8193, 16385, 24577
    • Direction 2: 8194, 16386, 24578
  • Other sketch entities (3D sketch (Unknown/SELOBJGROUP)
    • Direction 1: 12289, 24577
    • Direction 2: 16386, 28674
