Return Value

SOLIDWORKS background theme as defined in swInterfaceBrightnessTheme_e
Remarks

A SOLIDWORKS add-in can:

  • use this method to determine the current theme and colors of the SOLIDWORKS background.
  • be notified when the theme and colors in the SOLIDWORKS background change by registering for the DSldWorksEvents_InterfaceBrightnessThemeChangeNotifyEventHandler event.
